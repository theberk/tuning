    tuning

  • Mouth Peice.

    your mouth peice should be at least 5 inches in diameter.

  • Strings

    Regular guitar strings will work but make sure you have enough for both necks. Use a power drill to tune the top half to E-B-G-D-A-E. Then tune the bottom half E-A-D-G-B-E

  • barometer

    You will want to have your atmospheric preasure at 0.333 atmospheres or ATM. You can adjust your ATM using a hot rag to increase or a cold rag to decrease ATM.
